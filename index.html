Features

Everything your household needs
on one shelf-side screen

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Multi-user profiles

Add every member of the family. Each profile keeps its own allergens, illnesses, lifestyle preferences and budget — stored on your device.

🥜

Allergen guard

Presence-based matching across the FDA Big 9 and EU 14 allergens, powered by an ingredient alias dictionary that catches hidden triggers.

🩺

Condition thresholds

Diabetes, hypertension, kidney disease and more — numeric limits checked per 100g, with the most restrictive rule always winning.

🛒

Group purchases

Pool profiles into a group buy. One scan, a per-member breakdown, and a shared cart that respects everyone's constraints.

🔒

Locked parameters

Inside a group, nobody can edit your allergens or illnesses. Only you can change your own profile — the group updates automatically.

📡

Live barcode lookup

Type a barcode and Safe Shelf pulls ingredients and nutrition from Open Food Facts — 4M+ products from 150+ countries.
